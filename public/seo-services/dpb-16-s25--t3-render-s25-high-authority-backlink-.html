Deciphering the Metrics of High Authority Backlinks

When evaluating the effectiveness of a backlink service, it is vital to consider metrics associated with authority. Common frameworks include Domain Authority (DA) and Page Authority (PA) developed by Moz, which provide insight into how well a website can rank on search engine results pages. Here’s a quick overview:

Metric Scale Significance
Domain Authority (DA) 1-100 A higher score indicates greater potential for ranking.
Page Authority (PA) 1-100 Measures the likelihood of a specific page ranking above others.
Trust Flow (TF) 1-100 Indicates the trustworthiness of a link.
